FOMOdoro.in

FOMOdoro.in is a Pomodoro Timer that helps you stay focused and productive.

How to use:

  • You can customize timer durations in settings ⚙️
  • Select pomodoro mode (25 minutes)
  • Work until the timer rings
  • Take a short break (5 minutes)
  • After 4 pomodoros, take a longer break (15 minutes)
